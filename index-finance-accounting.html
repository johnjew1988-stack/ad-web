Finance & Accounting System Implementation

End-to-end implementation of enterprise finance and accounting platforms — covering general ledger, accounts payable and receivable, budgeting, financial reporting, and regulatory compliance management.

Core Finance Modules

General Ledger

Chart of accounts setup, journal entry automation, multi-currency support, and period-end close management.

Accounts Payable

Vendor invoice processing, three-way matching, payment scheduling, and early payment discount management.

Accounts Receivable

Customer invoicing, collections workflow, cash application, and aging analysis dashboards.

Budgeting & Forecasting

Annual budget planning, rolling forecasts, variance analysis, and departmental spend controls.

Financial Reporting

Balance sheet, income statement, cash flow statements, and custom management reports with drill-down capability.

Tax & Compliance

Automated tax calculations, audit trail management, IFRS/GAAP alignment, and regulatory filing support.

Fixed Assets

Asset register, depreciation scheduling, disposal tracking, and capital expenditure management.

Bank Reconciliation

Automated bank statement import, transaction matching, and exception-based reconciliation workflows.

Cost Accounting

Cost centre allocation, product costing, overhead absorption, and profitability analysis by segment.

Implementation Process

1. Requirements & Scoping

Finance process workshops, chart of accounts review, and integration requirements with existing ERP or systems.

2. System Design

Platform configuration, workflow design, approval hierarchies, and role-based access controls setup.

3. Data Migration

Historical financial data extraction, cleansing, mapping, and validation against source records.

4. Integration

Connect to banking feeds, payroll, procurement, and other enterprise systems via secure API or file-based exchange.

5. Testing & UAT

End-to-end scenario testing, parallel run with legacy system, and sign-off by finance team stakeholders.

6. Go-Live & Support

Phased cutover, user training, hypercare support window, and ongoing optimization cycles.
